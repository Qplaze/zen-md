# Privacy Policy — ZEN MD

**Last updated:** March 24, 2026

## Summary

ZEN MD does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Period.

---

## Data Collection

ZEN MD **does not collect** any data whatsoever. The application:

- Does not connect to the internet
- Does not send analytics or telemetry
- Does not use cookies or tracking technologies
- Does not collect personal information
- Does not access any data beyond the files you explicitly open

## Data Storage

ZEN MD stores a single configuration file locally on your device:

- **Location:** `%APPDATA%\ZenMD\settings.txt`
- **Contents:** Your display preferences only (theme, font, font size, column width)
- This file never leaves your device and is not transmitted anywhere

## File Access

ZEN MD only accesses Markdown files (`.md`, `.markdown`, `.txt`) that you explicitly open through the file dialog or drag-and-drop. No files are read, scanned, or accessed without your direct action.

## Third-Party Services

ZEN MD does not integrate with, connect to, or communicate with any third-party services, APIs, servers, or cloud platforms.

## Children's Privacy

ZEN MD does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be published alongside the application update. Since ZEN MD collects no data, material changes are unlikely.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at the project repository or contact the developer through the Microsoft Store listing.
